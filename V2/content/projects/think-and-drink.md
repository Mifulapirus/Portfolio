# Think and Drink Trivia

## Overview

Dive into the world of trivia where cold drinks meet hot questions, fostering friendships and fierce competition in the heart of Costa Rica.

## Features

- **Varied Themes**: From ancient mysteries, music, technology, mistranslations, colossal mistakes to nonsense facts, each week brings a new topic with well-researched and fun questions.
- **Great Locations**: Handpicked venues offering great food, amazing drinks, and the best trivia experience.
- **Expert Hosts**: Trivia enthusiasts with years of experience, ready to challenge your knowledge with the weirdest and most interesting questions.

## FAQs

- **What time do events start?** Kick-off times vary by location, but most begin around 5:30PM. Follow us to stay tuned!
- **Can anyone join?** Absolutely! Enthusiasts of all trivia levels are welcome. Prepare for a night of fun and challenge!
- **Is there an entry fee?** Events are free to join, but we encourage you to enjoy the food and drinks offered by our host venues!
- **What to bring to an event?** Just bring yourself, your friends, and your thirst for useless knowledge. We take care of the rest!
